Introduction

baxter's essential colour tools™ (BECT) is a collection of Baxter's favorite simple, color-related tools that would be useful for most designers and front-end developers. BECT has a wide selection of input methods, from HEX to a advanced colour picker. Not only does this application provide handy results, BECT also provides easy and instant conversion of different colour formats. While you type your RGB value, the other input methods, such as HEX, are converted in real time!
